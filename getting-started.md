# Getting started with {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}}
{: #gettingstartedtutorial}

## Step 1: Provisioning your {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}} service instance
{: #step1}

After you have created an instance of the OpenPages with Watson service at [https://cloud.ibm.com/catalog/services/openpages-with-watson](../catalog/services/openpages-with-watson), perform the following steps to provision that service.

1. In the **Getting started with {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}}** box, click **Go to OpenPages**. 
2. On the **Welcome** page, click **Next**.
3. On the **Select a solution module** page, select the module you want to explore and click **Continue to OpenPages**.
 
   The provisioning of your {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}} instance begins. This process should take less than five minutes. When it is done, click **Go to IBM OpenPages with Watson**.
4. Your instance of {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}} opens with the primary module you selected in step 3 as the starting point.
5. Close your OpenPages instance browser tab or log out of OpenPages.
6. Ensure that your instance of {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}} appears in your resource list.
 * In your web browser, go to [https://cloud.ibm.com/resources](../resources).
   Under **Services and software**, you should see the OpenPages instance you provisioned.
 * Click the instance. 
 * In the **Getting started with {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}}** box, click **Go to OpenPages**. Your instance of {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}} opens.
  
## Step 2: Exploring {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}}
{: #step2}

The **Getting started** tab has many links to help you explore {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}}. 

1. On the **Getting started** tab, click **Learn more** for any solution module you want to know more about.
2. Click any link in the **Quick navigation** list to move quickly around the {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}} interface.

## Step 3: Inviting users to join your account
{: #step3}

During the OpenPages Lite trial, you can add up to 9 additional users so that they can try out  {{site.data.keyword.openpages_short}} solutions.

1. On the **Getting started** tab, click **Edit user access** under **Trial users** on the right side of the tab.
2. In the **Invite** box of the **Edit collaborators** dialog box, enter the email addresses  of the users you want to invite. Ensure that the addresses are separated by commas.
3. Click **Invite**. The people you invited will get an email to join your account in IBM Cloud.

   After the process completes, the email addresses are listed at the bottom of the dialog box. A status, such as **Invitation pending**, is displayed for each email address.
